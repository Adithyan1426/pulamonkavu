Aluvilakku

Aluvilakku is a sacred lamp with 365 wicks, arranged in the shape of a **pipal tree**. It is lit using **pure ghee** as an offering to the divine. This ritual is believed to bring **prosperity, peace, and spiritual enlightenment** to devotees.

In many Shiva temples, lighting this lamp signifies **removal of darkness (ignorance) and welcoming divine blessings**. Devotees perform this offering for **good fortune, health, and fulfillment of wishes**.
