Aayilya Homam is a sacred fire ritual dedicated to the **serpent gods (Naga Devatas)**.

It is performed to remove Sarpa Dosha and seek protection from snake-related afflictions.

Significance: Helps in **ancestral blessings, prosperity, and health**.

When is it performed? On Aayilyam Nakshatra days, especially in temples like Mannarasala.
