Nithya Puja is a **daily worship ritual** performed to express devotion and gratitude to the divine.

It includes **lighting lamps, chanting mantras, offering flowers, and meditating**.

Significance: Helps in **spiritual growth, peace of mind, and divine blessings**.

When to Perform? Every day, preferably in the morning or evening, at home or in temples.
