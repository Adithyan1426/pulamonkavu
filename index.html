About Pulamankavu Sreemahadeva Temple

Pulamankavu Sreemahadeva Temple, located in Maranadu, Kerala, is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple's origins trace back several centuries, reflecting the rich spiritual heritage of the region.

Architecturally, it showcases traditional Kerala temple design, featuring intricate woodwork and a sloping tiled roof. The temple serves as a focal point for local festivals, especially during the annual Maha Shivaratri celebration, attracting devotees from various parts of the state.

The temple has played a significant role in preserving and promoting cultural and religious traditions. It continues to be a center for spirituality, art, and community gatherings.
